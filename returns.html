Returns & Exchanges

Our policy for returns, exchanges, and refunds

Important Note

All return/exchange requests must be initiated within 7 days of receiving your order. Please contact Miss Dora at 0595736002 before returning any items.

Eligibility for Returns/Exchanges

Eligible Items:

  • Items received damaged or defective
  • Wrong item shipped (different from what you ordered)
  • Size exchanges (if available stock exists)
  • Unopened and unused items with original packaging

Not Eligible:

  • Items used, worn, or washed
  • Items without original tags and packaging
  • Perfumes and opened beauty products (for hygiene reasons)
  • Sale or clearance items (unless defective)
  • Personalized or custom-made items

Return Process

1

Contact Us

Call/WhatsApp Miss Dora at 0595736002 within 7 days of receiving your order.

2

Get Approval

We'll assess your request and provide a return authorization if eligible.

3

Package Item

Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all tags attached.

4

Return Shipping

You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.

5

Inspection & Resolution

Once received, we'll inspect the item within 2-3 business days and process your exchange or refund.

Exchange Options

If you need a different size or color:

  • Size Exchange: Available if the desired size is in stock
  • Color Exchange: Subject to availability
  • Item Exchange: Can exchange for another item of equal or greater value
  • Exchange Time: Processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return

Note: Exchange shipping costs (both ways) are the customer's responsibility unless the exchange is due to our error.

Refund Policy

  • Refund Method: Refunds are issued via the original payment method
  • Processing Time: 5-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item
  • Deductions: Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Partial Refunds: May be issued for used or damaged items at our discretion
