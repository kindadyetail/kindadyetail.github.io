Delivery Process

At Kindady's E-tail, we ensure your orders are delivered with care and attention. Here's how our delivery process works:

  • Order Confirmation: After placing your order, you'll receive a confirmation via WhatsApp/call
  • Delivery Fee Discussion: We'll contact you to discuss the exact delivery fee based on your location
  • Processing: Orders are processed within 24-48 hours after payment confirmation
  • Delivery Time: 3-7 business days within Ghana, depending on your region
  • Tracking: You'll receive updates on your order status via WhatsApp
