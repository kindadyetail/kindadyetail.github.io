How to Measure Yourself

1

Bust/Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your bust/chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor

2

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline (usually the narrowest part of your torso)

3

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 20cm below your waist

4

Foot Length

Stand on a piece of paper, mark your heel and longest toe, then measure the distance

Tip: Always measure over undergarments, not over clothes. Use a soft measuring tape.
