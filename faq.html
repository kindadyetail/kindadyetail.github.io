Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Kindady's E-tail

Ordering Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place orders directly through our website by adding items to your cart and proceeding to checkout. Alternatively, you can WhatsApp Miss Dora at 0595736002 with the items you want to order.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. Contact us immediately at 0595736002. After 1 hour, orders enter processing and changes may not be possible.

Delivery Questions

How much is delivery and how is it calculated?

Delivery fees vary by region and are not fixed. You must contact Miss Dora at 0595736002 to discuss your specific delivery fee before placing your order. Fees depend on your location within Ghana.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by region:
• Greater Accra: 1-2 business days
• Other major cities: 2-4 business days
• Remote areas: 5-7 business days
All orders are processed within 24-48 hours after payment confirmation.

Payment Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:
• Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money)
• Cash on Delivery (available in select areas)
• Bank Transfer
Payment details are provided during checkout.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for defective, damaged, or wrong items. Items must be unused with original tags and packaging. Contact us at 0595736002 before returning any items.

Can I exchange an item for a different size?

Yes, size exchanges are available if the desired size is in stock. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility.
